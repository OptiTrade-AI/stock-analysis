Market Position

Industry Leadership

Amazon.com, Inc. is a diversified technology company with significant market presence in e-commerce, cloud computing (AWS), digital advertising, and emerging technologies. Founded in 1997 and headquartered in Seattle, Washington, Amazon employs over 1.5 million people worldwide and has a market capitalization of approximately $1.93 trillion.

Amazon's core e-commerce business accounts for approximately 75% of revenue, while AWS, contributing 15% of revenue, is the primary profit driver due to its high margins. The fast-growing advertising segment, representing 5-10% of revenue, is another high-margin business. Amazon is also investing heavily in AI, healthcare, and projects like Project Kuiper, a satellite internet initiative to compete with Starlink.

Recent news highlights Amazon's leadership in cloud computing with AWS holding a 31% global market share. The company's substantial investments in AI infrastructure, estimated at $100 billion in 2025, position it to capitalize on the growing demand for AI and cloud services. Despite a recent stock correction of 20-25% from all-time highs, analysts note Amazon's attractive valuation and potential for significant upside.
